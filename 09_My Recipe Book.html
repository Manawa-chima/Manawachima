Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients
200g spaghetti
100g pancetta
2 large eggs
50g grated Parmesan cheese
2 cloves garlic, minced
Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Reserve some pasta water.
  2. In a skillet, cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan in a bowl. Combine with pasta, pancetta, and reserved water off the heat.
  4. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients
200g butter
150g sugar
2 large eggs
300g flour
200g chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time, then stir in flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto a baking sheet and bake for 10-12 minutes.

Caesar Salad

Ingredients
1 large head of romaine lettuce
50g croutons
50g grated Parmesan cheese
Caesar dressing

Steps:

  1. Chop the romaine lettuce and place it in a large bowl.
  2. Add croutons and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing until well coated.
  4. Serve immediately with an extra sprinkle of cheese.
